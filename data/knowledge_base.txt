CLARITYCS KNOWLEDGE BASE — Customer Success Operations Manual
Last Updated: January 2026

=== ESCALATION POLICY ===

CRITICAL tickets (P1):
- First response required within 1 hour
- Engineering must be looped in within 2 hours if unresolved
- Customer update cadence: every 30 minutes
- Team lead must be notified immediately
- If unresolved after 4 hours: VP CS notified, executive sponsor contacted

HIGH tickets (P2):
- First response required within 4 hours
- Engineering loop-in if unresolved after 8 hours
- Customer update cadence: every 2 hours

MEDIUM tickets (P3):
- First response required within 1 business day
- Resolution target: 3 business days

LOW tickets (P4):
- First response required within 2 business days
- Resolution target: 5 business days

=== CHURN RISK INDICATORS ===

Automatic escalation to CSM required when ANY of the following occur:
- 3 or more Critical tickets within 90 days
- Customer mentions cancel, terminate, competitor, switch, legal
- CSAT score below 3 for two consecutive tickets
- No login activity for 14+ days on Pro/Enterprise plans
- Outstanding invoice over 30 days
- Contract renewal within 60 days AND open Critical ticket
- C-suite directly contacts support (CEO, CFO, CTO, COO)

=== RESOLUTION QUALITY STANDARDS ===

A high-quality resolution must include:
1. Root cause identified and documented
2. Immediate fix confirmed working by customer
3. Permanent fix timeline communicated if different from immediate fix
4. Preventative measures documented to avoid recurrence
5. CSAT follow-up sent within 24 hours

Poor resolution indicators:
- Issue closed without root cause identification
- Customer re-opens same ticket within 7 days
- CSAT score of 1 or 2 without follow-up action
- Resolution notes say only issue resolved with no detail

=== REFUND AND CREDIT POLICY ===

Automatic credit approved (no manager sign-off needed):
- Billing errors under $500
- Service downtime exceeding SLA (pro-rated credit)
- Feature bug causing data loss (full month credit)

Manager approval required:
- Credits over $500
- Full month or more of credit
- Refunds on annual plans

VP approval required:
- Credits over $5000
- Refunds on Enterprise contracts

=== ENTERPRISE ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT ===

Enterprise accounts (over $10000 ARR) receive:
- Dedicated CSM assigned
- Monthly check-in calls
- Quarterly Business Reviews (QBR)
- Priority routing to senior agents
- Custom SLA terms as per contract

QBR content requirements:
- SLA performance last quarter
- Open and resolved ticket summary
- Feature adoption metrics
- Renewal date and ARR at risk
- Recommended next steps

=== DATA AND COMPLIANCE ===

HIPAA-covered customers: MediTrack Health, Orbis Healthcare, BrightPath Education
- All tickets flagged HIPAA must be handled by certified agents only
- Data access logs retained for minimum 7 years
- No PHI in ticket descriptions — use case reference numbers only

GDPR customers: All EU-based accounts
- Data deletion requests: 30-day SLA
- Data export requests: 72-hour SLA
- Incident notification: 72-hour regulatory requirement

=== COMMUNICATION TEMPLATES ===

Critical Incident Opening:
Thank you for reaching out. I understand the urgency and I am making this my immediate priority. I will have an update for you within the committed timeframe. In the meantime, I am taking immediate action to investigate.

Escalation Notice:
I have escalated this to our engineering team and they are actively investigating. Your account has been flagged as Priority 1. I will update you within 30 minutes.

Resolution Confirmation:
I am pleased to confirm this has been resolved. Root cause identified. Fix applied and confirmed. Preventative measures in place. Please confirm everything is working as expected.
